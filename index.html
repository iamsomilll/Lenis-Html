ROHIT SHARMA

test cricketer

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer who plays for the Indian national team in Test cricket. He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional right-arm off break bowler. Rohit is known for his exceptional batting skills and leadership qualities. After facing initial struggles in Test cricket, Rohit made a remarkable comeback and established himself as a key player in the format, showcasing his resilience and determination.
